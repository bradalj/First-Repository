No matter whose money you are working with – yours, your company’s or your client’s – having reliable, accurate and secure reporting and data management systems are critical in the financial industry.

We offer support in multiple languages and currencies and also have experience confronting the most far-reaching security and PII threats. Working in a variety of finance-related sectors – insurance, high-speed securities trading and even personal financial management – ExtensionEngine has the experience to protect your data and support your most mission-critical operations.

At ExtensionEngine, we partner with you to determine your specific technical needs and create a software solution that will quickly deal with your concerns. Whether you need to take data and change it from information to actionable business intelligence, determine how to better deliver usability to your customers, or integrate the constantly changing financial regulations into your daily work, we can partner with you to make your systems run seamlessly.

Once a comprehensive solution to your challenge has been developed, we will work with you, as part of your team, to implement the solution that makes sense given your particular organizational requirements. With our team of software engineers and their commitment to make your business work, ExtensionEngine will take your complex technical challenges and create a reliable, flexible solution.
