ABOUT US

Since it was founded in 2000, extensionEngine has worked with clients to deliver elegant and cost-effective solutions to complex technology and business challenges. Our focus is to successfully develop solutions to some of the world’s most difficult problems for some of the world’s most dynamic companies.

Headquartered in Boston, with a European development center located in Split, Croatia, extensionEngine assists clients utilizing our blended onshore/offshore model. With a pool of engineering talent in Croatia and UX/UI designers and business analysts, extensionEngine provides comprehensive, broad-based solutions. We hire the most talented people that we can find, offer them interesting and rewarding work, and treat them as a team.

The result? Our product development knowledge, technical expertise, project management capabilities and ability to execute provides our clients with the reliable, long-term software solutions they need. extensionEngine's architects and engineers specialize in rapid software development, enabling profitable and sustainable growth for our clients. We leverage the resources of not only our home office, but the innovations of our development center and quickly apply those solutions as our team works on-site.

Profitable every year since our founding, extensionEngine boasts a turnover percentage of less than 5% over the last five years. With steady growth, a passion for creating the most effective software solutions, and a team approach, extensionEngine offers our clients remarkable service and products.
