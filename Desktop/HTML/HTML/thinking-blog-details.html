Not Your Father’s IBM!

Nov 22, 2011 by BOB ALLARD

IT services will be a bedrock of business, and that no business can be run without quality computers and software. In this realm, IBM is supreme with its innovative focus on business solutions, a strategy that Wharton marketing professor George S. Day says “is sound and hard to copy.” Even though IBM’s transformation may have finally endeared it to Buffett, the fabled investor’s final embrace of technology still is a recognition that IT firms play a crucial role in powering business and will likely do so for the foreseeable future. And that is a view that we at extensionEngine share with Mr. Buffett.
